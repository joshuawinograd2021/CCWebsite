Our Mission

Community Currency collects wasted foreign currency to chip away at educational inequalities. Through a partnership with Boys & Girls Clubs of Chicago, our proceeds send disadvantaged Chicagoland children to BGCC’s summer programs.

What We Do

Step 1:
We collect wasted foreign currency in international
airports, college study abroad offices, and banks.


Step 2:
The currency is converted.


Step 3:
Proceeds go directly to Boys and Girls Clubs of Chicago.
